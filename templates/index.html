      Maritime Research Centre



      Dedicated towards Underwater Domain Awareness


      As a think tank and a registered not-for-profit
      organization (Section-8 company),the Maritime Research Center (MRC)
      is dedicated towards promotion of Underwater Domain Awareness (UDA).
      Over the years, MRC has emerged as a platform of national and
      regional significance, fostering dialogue and cooperation amongst
      stakeholders from Maritime Security,Blue Economy, Marine Environment,
      Disaster Management and Research & Innovation.
